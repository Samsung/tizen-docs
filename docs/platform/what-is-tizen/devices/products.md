# Tizen Products

## Seamless Experience, Core Intelligence — Tizen OS

Tizen OS powers a wide range of Samsung products — from TVs and monitors to digital signage and home appliances.

It provides a consistent, reliable platform that connects devices seamlessly and delivers a unified experience across different categories.

Through continuous evolution in performance, security, and scalability, Tizen OS remains the foundation of Samsung’s smart device ecosystem.

## TV and Monitor

Tizen OS powers millions of Smart TVs and monitors worldwide.

It offers advanced graphics performance, responsive UI, and long-term software support for an exceptional visual experience.

## Digital Signage and Commercial Displays

Designed for professional environments such as retail, education, and public spaces, Tizen OS provides a stable, high-performance platform optimized for continuous operation and large-scale display environments.

## Smart Home Appliances

From refrigerators and washing machines to ovens and other connected home devices, Tizen OS provides an intuitive and stable interface.

It allows multiple devices within a home to work together smoothly, forming the core of a connected lifestyle.
